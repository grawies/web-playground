content entity references

They are basically encodings of symbols that usually are "reserved or non-standard" in HTML. Basically anything with an & - ampersand. They can safeguard against more limited character encoding, help avoid rendering issues, and they provide characters not available on a keyboard. Don't be afraid to be < than a 100 % success & > more!
